# Lisbon — April 2026

4 days in April. Probably the best city trip I've done.

## Highlights

**Alfama** — the old Moorish quarter. Go early morning before the tour groups. The Miradouro da Graça at sunrise is worth the early alarm.

**LX Factory** — repurposed industrial space with good food, independent shops, and a Sunday market.

**Pastéis de Belém** — the original custard tarts since 1837. The queue moves fast, worth it every time.

**Mouraria** — less touristic than Alfama, great neighbourhood restaurants.

## Practical notes

- Transport: Viva Viagem card covers metro, trams, and buses. Get the 24h pass.
- Tram 28: skip it for transport (slow, packed), take it once for the experience.
- Best dinner: A Cevicheria — book ahead.
- Walk everywhere — the city is hilly but compact.

## Would skip

The Fado shows on the main tourist strips feel staged. Find a local tasca instead.
