# Mushroom Risotto

**Serves:** 4 · **Time:** 35 min

## Ingredients

- 300g arborio rice
- 250g mixed mushrooms (cremini, shiitake)
- 1L warm vegetable stock
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 100ml dry white wine
- 60g parmesan, grated
- 30g butter
- Olive oil, salt, pepper

## Method

1. Sauté onion in olive oil over medium heat until soft, \~5 min.
2. Add garlic and mushrooms, cook until golden.
3. Add rice, toast 2 min. Pour in wine and stir until absorbed.
4. Add stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until absorbed before adding more (\~20 min).
5. Remove from heat, stir in butter and parmesan. Season well.

> Add a handful of fresh thyme with the mushrooms for extra depth.
