# Pasta Carbonara

**Serves:** 2 · **Time:** 20 min

## Ingredients

- 200g spaghetti or rigatoni
- 100g guanciale or pancetta, diced
- 3 egg yolks + 1 whole egg
- 60g pecorino romano, grated
- Black pepper, salt

## Method

1. Cook pasta in well-salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Render guanciale in a dry pan until crispy. Remove from heat.
3. Whisk eggs with pecorino and a generous amount of black pepper.
4. Add drained pasta to pan (off heat). Pour egg mixture over, toss vigorously, adding pasta water gradually to get a creamy sauce.

> Never add the eggs over direct heat — they'll scramble. The residual heat from the pasta is enough.
